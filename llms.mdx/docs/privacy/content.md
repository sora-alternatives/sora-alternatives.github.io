# Privacy Policy (/docs/privacy)



Privacy Policy [#privacy-policy]

**Last Updated:** April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sora Alternatives ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit soraalternatives.com (the "Site").

1\. Information We Collect [#1-information-we-collect]

1.1 Information You Provide [#11-information-you-provide]

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

* Contact us via email or contact forms
* Subscribe to newsletters or notifications
* Leave comments or participate in discussions

1.2 Automatically Collected Information [#12-automatically-collected-information]

When you visit the Site, we may automatically collect:

* **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and access times
* **Device Information:** Hardware model, screen resolution, and device identifiers
* **Usage Data:** How you interact with the Site, including clicks, scroll depth, and time spent on pages

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies [#13-cookies-and-tracking-technologies]

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

* Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
* Remember your preferences and settings
* Improve website performance and user experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

2\. How We Use Your Information [#2-how-we-use-your-information]

We use the collected information to:

* Operate and maintain the Site
* Analyze usage trends to improve our content and user experience
* Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
* Send newsletters or updates (only if you have opted in)
* Comply with legal obligations

3\. Third-Party Services [#3-third-party-services]

3.1 Analytics [#31-analytics]

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect and analyze usage data. These services may use cookies and similar technologies.

3.2 Affiliate Links [#32-affiliate-links]

The Site contains affiliate links to third-party AI video services. When you click on an affiliate link:

* You may be redirected to the third-party's website
* The third-party may set their own cookies
* We may receive a commission if you make a purchase

Third-party websites are governed by their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

4\. Data Sharing [#4-data-sharing]

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytical purposes.

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Site (e.g., hosting, analytics), subject to confidentiality agreements.

5\. Data Retention [#5-data-retention]

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6\. Your Rights [#6-your-rights]

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

* Access the personal information we hold about you
* Request correction or deletion of your personal information
* Object to or restrict the processing of your personal information
* Request data portability
* Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

7\. Data Security [#7-data-security]

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8\. Children's Privacy [#8-childrens-privacy]

The Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9\. International Transfers [#9-international-transfers]

If you access the Site from outside our primary operating region, your information may be transferred to and processed in other jurisdictions. By using the Site, you consent to such transfers.

10\. Changes to This Privacy Policy [#10-changes-to-this-privacy-policy]

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

11\. Contact Us [#11-contact-us]

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at &#x2A;*[support@soraalternatives.com](mailto:support@soraalternatives.com)** or visit our [Contact Us](/docs/contact) page.
